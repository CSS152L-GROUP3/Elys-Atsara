PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: 5/15/2025

At Ely's Atsara, we are committed to safeguarding the privacy and security of our customers' personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the data you provide when using our website. We respect your right to privacy and ensure that any information you share with us is treated with the utmost care and responsibility.

When you place an order or register an account on our website, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, contact number, shipping and billing addresses, and basic payment details. We also gather non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit on our website to help improve our services and user experience.

The information we collect is used solely for purposes related to order processing, customer support, promotional communications (with your consent), and enhancing the functionality and security of our website. We may share necessary information with trusted third parties, such as payment processors and delivery partners like J&T Express, but only for the purpose of fulfilling your orders efficiently. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any other parties.
