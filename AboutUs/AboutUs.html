The Developers

Vincent DC Lusung (UI/UX developer) - is responsible for the User Experience (UX) and User Interface (UI) design of the system. He ensures that the layout, visuals, and overall design are intuitive, engaging, and accessible to users. His role involves creating wireframes, mockups, and prototypes that guide the frontend development, while also focusing on delivering a seamless and enjoyable user journey throughout the platform.

Feone Remoquillo (Frontend Developer) - leads the frontend development of the website application. She transforms the UI designs into responsive and functional web pages using modern frameworks and technologies. Her tasks include implementing user-facing features, optimizing performance across different devices, and ensuring the interface is both aesthetically consistent and highly interactive.

Anna Mikaela Gavieres (Backend Developer) - is in charge of backend development, where she builds the core logic and server-side functionalities that power the system. Her work includes managing APIs, processing user requests, handling authentication, and ensuring that data flows smoothly between the client interface and the database. She plays a crucial role in maintaining the reliability and security of the system.

Jase Asuncion (Database Manager) - takes care of the database management and architecture. He designs the structure of data storage, ensures proper normalization, and implements secure methods for handling data. His responsibilities include creating efficient queries, maintaining data integrity, and supporting the backend with consistent and reliable data access to enhance the overall performance of the website application.
